CYBR 4350: Senior Project

A browser-based Security Operations Center for hands-on training. Work realistic incidents across a live SIEM, a global threat map, incident response, and threat intelligence. Triage, contain, document, and deliver a post-mortem your CISO can brief. One hundred graded shift scenarios plus a 20-challenge Capture the Flag arena.

Designed and authored by Professor Rizwan Virani, San Jacinto College.

Sign in for this shift

Your name is used to generate the Blackboard completion code when you finish a shift. Progress saves to this browser.

New here? Start in three steps

  1. Enter your name above and click Save. It tags the completion codes you turn in to Blackboard.
  2. Pick a scenario from a tier below. Tier 1 is triage and the best place to begin. Tap a tier to open its list of shifts.
  3. Work the incident on the workstation. Read the brief, watch the live SIEM feed, investigate, follow the playbook, then submit your findings and the required deliverables.

What this is

  • A hands-on SOC analyst trainer. You triage a live SIEM feed, work real incidents, and write the deliverables a working analyst produces.
  • Built for CYBR 4350. Every finished shift gives a completion code you turn in to Blackboard.
  • Self-paced and saved to this browser. Pick any tier, any time, and come back where you left off.

What this is not

  • Not a real network. Every host, log, and attacker is simulated for training. No live systems are touched.
  • Not a hacking tool. The focus is defense, detection, and response, not offense.
  • Not graded by clicks alone. The in-app work is step one of five. The written deliverables are the rest.
