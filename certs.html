Exam facts and roles

CySA+ V4 launched June 23, 2026. The exam has up to 85 multiple-choice and performance-based questions, runs 165 minutes, and is scored on a 100 to 900 scale with 750 to pass. The U.S. voucher is 425 dollars. It is approved under DoD Directive 8140.
